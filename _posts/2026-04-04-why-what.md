I'm Meetkumar Naik — a Mechatronics engineer based in Perth, perpetually curious about how things move and how to make them move better.

I'm building this portfolio for three reasons: to keep my skills sharp, to learn new ones, and to create a body of work I'm proud of.

The work will span robotics, AI and engineering projects across land, air and water. Autonomous systems, control theory, computer vision, embedded electronics — the full stack of making things move intelligently. Over the 
next three years the goal is six demonstratable projects.

Between larger projects, I also intend to document learnings and side quests related to smaller projects. 

SETPOINT is where the thinking lives. The code lives on GitHub. I can be contacted via my email address listed on my Github and LinkedIn pages.
